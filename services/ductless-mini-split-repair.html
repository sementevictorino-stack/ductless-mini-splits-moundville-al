Common Ductless Mini Split Problems We Repair

Our repair services cover the full spectrum of ductless mini split issues that can affect system performance and efficiency. One of the most common problems we encounter is refrigerant leaks, which can significantly impact cooling and heating performance while potentially causing long-term damage to system components. Our technicians use advanced leak detection equipment to locate even small leaks and perform professional repairs using approved materials and techniques.

Electrical problems are another frequent cause of ductless system malfunctions. Issues with control boards, sensors, fan motors, and wiring can cause a variety of symptoms including failure to start, intermittent operation, or complete system shutdown. Our technicians have the specialized tools and knowledge needed to diagnose electrical problems safely and perform repairs that restore reliable operation.

Drainage issues can also affect ductless mini split performance, particularly in Alabama's humid climate. Clogged condensate drains, damaged drain pans, or malfunctioning condensate pumps can lead to water damage and system shutdowns. Our repair services include thorough cleaning of drainage systems and replacement of damaged components to prevent future problems.
